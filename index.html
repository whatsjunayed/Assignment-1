Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh has been seeing many innovations in recent years, including in the startup ecosystem, environmental solutions, and ICT.

Sustainability

Bangladesh is focusing on sustainable development, renewable energy, climate resilience, and eco-friendly practices for growth.

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is improving infrastructure with modern roads, bridges, ports, and urban development for economic growth.

Empowerment

Bangladesh focuses on women’s empowerment, education, healthcare, and entrepreneurship to drive inclusive socio-economic development.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.

To solve the problem of poverty, we have to recognize that poverty is not just about the lack of money, it is about the lack of opportunities. People don’t want charity, they want the opportunity to work, to support themselves, and to live with dignity. Through microcredit, we have shown that poor people can be given the tools to start businesses, support their families, and create sustainable livelihoods. This is not a handout, it is an investment in human potential.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus

Chief Adviser of the People's Republic of Bangladesh
